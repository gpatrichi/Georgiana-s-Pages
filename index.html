Georgiana's Romanian Meatball Soup

Traditional Recipe with a Twist




The history of the soup

This soup is very yummy. My mom used to make it every summer when I was a kid. It's hydrating and it's chock full of veggies and protein, which make it a very healthy soup.


List of ingredients

For this soup, you are going to need the following ingredients:

Soup base:

  • 3 carrots
  • 1 potato (sweet potato is also OK)
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/2 parsnip root
  • 1/2 celery root
  • 1 bell pepper (any color)
  • 1/2 bunch lovage
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • juice from 2 lemons

Meatballs:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 2 tbsp white rice
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 bunch parsley
  • 1/2 bunch dill
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste

Directions

Preparing this soup can be a bit time-consuming, so make sure you give yourself enough prep time.

Steps for prepping the veggies:

  1. First, wash, clean, peel, and chop the vegetables (I use a food processor to save time).
  2. Next, add all the veggies to a large pot.
  3. Add the lovage and black pepper.
  4. Add 3l of water to the pot.
  5. Set stove to High and bring to a boil.

While the veggies are beginning to cook, start prepping the meatballs.


Steps for prepping the meatballs:

  1. Finely dice the parsley and dill.
  2. Add the ground turkey, rice, egg, herbs, paprika, salt, and pepper to a large bowl.
  3. Mix ingredients well by hand (I usuall wear food-grade medical gloves to avoid contact with skin).
  4. Using your hands, form 2-inch diameter ball-shaped meatballs (for a more consistent size, you can use a melon scooper).
  5. As the veggies are coming to a boil, start slowly placing the meatballs in the soup one by one.

Steps for cooking:

  1. Allow all soup ingredients to come to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, turn the stove down to medium-low.
  3. Cover pot with a lid and let cook for approximately 30 minutes.
  4. Stir the soup occasionally to ensure meatballs cook evenly.
  5. When finished cooking, turn off stove and add salt and the lemon juice.
  6. Stir well and let sit for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve with Greek yogurt or sour cream, a hot pepper, and fresh bread.

Enjoy!
